English Bulldogs

History, Health Issues, and Sources

History

  • The Bulldog was trained and bred to fight bulls for sport, beginning in England in the 1200s and throughout Europe until the mid 1800s
  • Both the Bulldog and the Mastiff have a common ancestor: The Alaunt
  • Bull-Baiting was performed by bulldogs to tenderize the beef, butchers who sold non-baited beef were subject to penalty as the beef was improper for consumption

Health Precautions

  1. Keep facial and other body wrinkles dry and clean, to help prevent skin fold dermatitis
  2. This breed commonly requires a Caesarean delivery for giving birth -- consult your veterinarian before considering breeding your Bulldog
  3. This breed commonly suffers from complications while under anesthesia because of their unusual airways -- you will want to address this concern with your veterinarian should your Bulldog require any surgeries
